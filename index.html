About me!

I am a musician, a people's person, a trying optimist, a lover of food and I love connecting with and learning more about awesome people, whether it's family, friends, or strangers! I often spend my time making music - writing, recording, and producing songs. I explore and blend a variety of different genres in my music, including rock, soul/r&b, blues, and dance music. If I'm not creating or dancing to music, you'll probably find me spending time with friends and family, eating food, or watching youtube videos on history, science, philosophy, and politics.
